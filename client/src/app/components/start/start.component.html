About Us

We are the team in charge of managing the point-of-sale system of each of the PriceSmart clubs in Central America, the Caribbean, and South America. At present we have a group of 14 people distributed in the United States, Guatemala, and Colombia. Each member of the team complies with specific tasks to ensure the proper functioning of the system and to support the requirements of each country, offering tailored solutions and seeking to be at the forefront in order to contribute to the improvement of the service we offer to our members.
