About Us

We are a Cali company, born to provide innovative solutions through ICT in the Colombian agricultural sector, especially the coconut sector. We are confident that through innovative solutions, we will help and promote the growth of a sector with enormous potential in the national and international scene. We provide software development solutions using state-of-the-art technology, committing ourselves to deliver products that meet the needs of the sector. Our team, aligned with the sector, knows the virtues and deficiencies of the sector, so we are able to provide the best response to each of the problems and needs.


E-Commerce

E-Commerce, from any field, has shown incredible growth in the commercialization of technological products, textiles, foodstuffs, etc. For this reason, Agrosmart is committed to the implementation of an E-Commerce platform that allows industrialists and producers to have direct contact, fostering and facilitating growth, visibility, confidence, sales, and business in the sector.

Agro Solutions

In addition to platforms such as E-Commerce, Agrosmart focuses its vision on contributing to the agricultural sector, solutions that generate trust and facilitate, to those interested, the creation of strategies that allow creating a focus on growth. For that, Agro-Smart includes within its innovation portfolio, solutions such as Agro-Statistics, Irrigation Systems and Pest Detection Systems.

Desing Industrial

Every day, every agroindustrial entrepreneur requires machinery to speed up their production processes of coconut-based products. That is why Agrosmart is betting (with its industrial design team) on the design and creation of implements for the creation and transformation of the coconut, offering economic and indispensable industrial equipment for the execution of these processes.
