    Project: ASC02

    Name: Coconut Sector Statistics.

    This project consists of the elaboration of a system to store, process and show in an appropriate way, the data collected from the entire Coco production chain in the Colombian Pacific.

    This will allow to have a better visibility of the sector and thus take opportune decisions that favor the growth of the chain and position it among the most competitive in the world.

    Project: ASC03

    Name: Knowledge management of cocotera communities.

    Currently the sector has problems in the management of knowledge they have about the cultivation of            Coconut,            in which ancestral practices are followed that are different for each region.

    What is intended is to take this knowledge and develop a set of good practices that are hosted in a centralized information system and that any person that is part of the productive chain of the coconut can access this information without any problem from any device. In this way we will preserve the knowledge of this beautiful work and it will be possible to transmit to future generations in a more didactic and easy to learn way.

    Project: ASC01

    Name: Marketing platform from coconut farming to industry.

    In the coconut sector there is a great problem that occurs when producers can sell their products in an appropriate way which causes many of the producing farms to lose their crops.

    In order to attack this problem, a technological platform will be created that allows farmers to sell your crops directly to industrial companies or buyers of any kind who are interested in the Coconut farming if need to have intermediaries.

    Project: ASC04

    Name: Certification of Production Farms

    The processes that concern the certification of production farms are an important pillar for the  growth of the sector since in this way crops can be exported to other countries, which will allow  obtain greater profits and more demand for the product.

    To achieve this goal, a platform will be created in which all the processes and formats that will be centralized will be centralized. are required to achieve the certification of a farm, all this accompanied by our team of consultants specialized in the subject.
