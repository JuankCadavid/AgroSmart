How was Agrosmart born?

A great opportunity

Agrosmart is an idea that was born in response to obvious flaws and problems in the coconut sector. Due to our direct interaction with the sector and the knowledge our team has about the coconut processing processes (from harvest to consumption), we identify that the sector can reach its maximum potential by directly relying on technology.
For this reason, we decided to establish ourselves as a team to analyze and propose solutions that allow us to generate growth and development in the sector. We are confident that our work will allow us to provide and implement solutions that are finally consolidated as opportunities for improvement.
